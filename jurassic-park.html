Jurassic Park (Stern, 2019)

Last updated: 2024-02-18

Based on code v1.13

  1. The basic idea - get T-Rex and Control room modes as fast as possible to cash in big points. Start Dino paddocks when possible and try to always have one running in the background
  2. Depending on the rubber position on a short plunge, try to get to the right flipper without qualifying the playfield. Aim for the "C" shot during ball save so that you're making progress towards C-H-A-O-S
  3. Get the truck facing to the right so that you're heading towards the Brachiosaurus paddock. I like this one when first learning JP since you get some extra points off of rescues. If you're looking to blow the game up, take the Gallimimus on the left instead.
  4. Complete M-A-P (shown on the back board above the pops) via any solid WHITE shots. Starting out you just need the P which is most easily done via: left ramp -> ski pass (might require a little nudge up) -> left ramp to start the dino paddock.
  5. In the paddock, hit the truck enough times to start Feed T-Rex hurry-up. If you're after points, shoot everything except the left ramp to cash in some points and build the T-Rex Multiball jackpot value.
  6. Start the multiball on the left ramp, and use it to more safely finish the dino paddock
    • Make at least one RESCUE shot
    • Shoot one of the two SET TRAP targets behind the truck
    • Shot one of the two flashing, roving YELLOW shots to capture the dino
    Subsequent dino paddocks will grow in complexity, but still follow this basic format. As you get more used to the dino paddocks on JP, you can delay using T-Rex Multiball to the second or third paddock, depending on how comfortable you are with the machine's setup. For humongous points in T-Rex Multiball, shoot the RED jackpot from left to right. Doing them in this order adds +1x to the following jackpot. The "Supply Drop" target is your add-a-ball, if it's lit.
  7. After capturing the Dino, the Control Room is ready. Shoot it and select "System Boot" all the way to the right. This is the easiest mode to complete and is worth a lot.
  8. Shoot two-way "combos" to finish System Boot - just follow the lights. The final shot is at the control room after hitting the three two-way combos; so seven shots total to finish the mode.
  9. If you're still alive at this point - prioritize hitting the truck to qualify more T-Rex modes and work through them in order if you can - Chase, Encounter, Rampage. You can control which T-Rex event you go into via full flips of the truck
  10. For each T-Rex mode, you'll do just fine following the flashing lights, but it's worth your time to look up their rules once you get a basic feel for them and are consistently reaching these modes
